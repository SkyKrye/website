This is the space-related section of the website! This section has my astrophotographs, gear and much more! This section will have more content soon and just enjoy for now!

Frequently Asked Questions:
> What do I use? The first tools I had for astronomy were my mom's phone, a tripod and a 28x monocular. It's zoom nor light collecting power was great but it worked for the time. Later on my aunt had an old Nikon L27 lying around so she gave it to me and that's what I've been using and still do (for now). I really hope I'm able to get a good telescope, that'll really boost everything for me.
> What do I edit my images with? I don't edit my images a lot now but when I do, I use Paint.NET (not the website!).
